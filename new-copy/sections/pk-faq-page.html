Shipping

Assembly & Installation

Yes, all our cabinets include the necessary assembly hardware and soft-close hinges. However, specific wall-mounting screws may need to be purchased separately based on your wall type.
We offer both options. Our standard shipping is for RTA (Ready-To-Assemble) cabinets, which are easier to ship and more cost-effective. Pre-assembly services are available for local orders.
While we specialize in manufacturing and distribution, we have a network of preferred contractors in Southern California. Contact our trade desk for recommendations.
For RTA assembly, you will typically need a screwdriver (or power drill), wood glue, and a rubber mallet. For installation, a level, shims, and a stud finder are essential.

Returns

Unopened items in their original packaging can be returned within 30 days of delivery. A 20% restocking fee applies to all returns, and the customer is responsible for return shipping.
To start a return, please contact our support team with your order number. We will provide you with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) and shipping instructions.
Please inspect your delivery immediately. If you notice damage, note it on the delivery receipt and contact us within 48 hours. We will ship replacement parts at no cost.
Sample doors are non-refundable, but the cost of your sample can be applied as a credit toward your full kitchen order (minimum purchase applies).
